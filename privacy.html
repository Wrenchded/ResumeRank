Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At ResumeRank AI, we take your privacy seriously. This document explains how we handle your data when you use our AI-powered resume audit service.

The Golden Rule: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or resume data to third parties.

1. Data Processing

When you submit a resume and job description, the data is processed via Google Gemini 3 Flash. This is used solely to generate your Match Score and Gap Analysis.

2. Information We Collect

We collect your email address only to deliver the final report. We do not maintain a permanent database of resumes after the email has been successfully sent.

3. AI Ethics

Our AI analysis is designed to be objective. We encourage users to use the feedback as a guide and not the sole factor in their career decisions.

4. Security

All data transfers are encrypted. Your submissions are handled within the secure Google Cloud environment via Apps Script.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact Adi Sarmalkar through the GitHub repository for this project.
